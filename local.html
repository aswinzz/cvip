How To Reach

  • Allahabad Airport is connected to major cities. Plan a flight trip from your nearest city to Allahabad Airport (IXD). After reaching Allahabad Airport book a Cab/Taxi/Auto to reach IIIT - Allahabad, Jhalwa, Allahabad. You can book cab online through Ola.
  • If you don't get flight directly to Allahabad, then you can also plan a flight trip to Vanarasi Airport (VNS). After reaching Varanasi Airport book a Cab/Taxi to reach Varanasi Cantt. In Varanasi Cantt. you can either opt for train or bus to reach Allahabad. After reaching Allahabad book a cab/taxi/auto to reach IIIT - Allahabad, Jhalwa, Allahabad.You can also readily find Auto in Allahabad Junction to Jhalwa with minimum share-fare. You can book cab online through Ola.
  • Trains are readily available from Delhi to Allahabad Junction (Average travel time is 7 Hrs). After reaching Allahabad Junction, book a Auto/Cab to reach IIIT-Allahabad, Jhalwa. The distance from Allahabad Junction to IIIT Allahabad is 7-8 KMs.

Accomodation

Accommodation for the participants (Student/Faculty/Industry Person) will be arranged in the Visitor Hostels (Guest House) of IIIT-Allahabad Jhalwa Campus, on payment basis which will be facilitated on a first come first serve.
